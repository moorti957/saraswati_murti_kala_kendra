The Workshop
Where stone becomes spirit

A Legacy Carved in Stone

Founded in 1985 by Master Sculptor Shri Rameshwar Prasad in the heart of Jaipur, Saraswati Murti Kala Kendra began as a small workshop with a grand vision — to keep the ancient art of marble sculpting alive while serving the spiritual needs of devotees worldwide.

Today, under the guidance of the third generation, our team of 25+ skilled artisans continues to create masterpieces from premium Makrana marble — the same marble that was used to build the Taj Mahal.
